CODM Season 13: What's New?

June 20, 2024

The latest update for Call of Duty Mobile introduces the "Crimson Shadow" operator, a brand new Battle Pass filled with exclusive skins and rewards, and the highly anticipated "Nightfall" multiplayer map. Experience the revamped weapon arsenal with gameplay improvements across the board.

Buffs: Enhancing Your Arsenal

June 18, 2024

The RUS-79U SMG gets improved recoil stability and faster reload times, perfect for close-range combat. The Arctic .50 sniper rifle now has increased damage over distance. Plus, Ajax’s sprint recovery speed has been boosted to keep you in the fight longer.

Nerfs: Balancing the Battlefield

June 16, 2024

The MSMC SMG’s firing rate has been slightly reduced and the AUG assault rifle’s hip-fire accuracy has been nerfed. Additionally, UAV scorestreak duration dropped from 60 to 45 seconds, helping balance map control.
